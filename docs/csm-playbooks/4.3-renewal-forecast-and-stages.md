**SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW**

| **Purpose** | Provide CSMs and CX leadership with a shared framework for forecasting renewals, tracking accounts through renewal stages, and calculating weighted ARR forecasts for executive reporting. |
| --- | --- |
| **Who This Is For** | All CSMs. Reviewed weekly by CSMs and CX leadership in team meetings. |
| **How It's Used** | Q1/Q2 forecast is built from CSM-updated SFDC renewal categories. Q3/Q4 forecast is built from Health Scores. Both feed the weekly weighted forecast that leadership reports to the business. |
| **Escalation Path** | CSM → Anna → Sarah |
| **Key Rule** | All renewal notes live in Chatter (SFDC) — NOT Slack. |

**SECTION 2 — RENEWAL FORECAST CATEGORIES**

**ℹ** These four categories are the only valid forecast values in SFDC. Every account with a renewal in the next 12 months must have one assigned. Update weekly.

| **Forecast Category** | **Guidelines** | **Indicators** | **Weighted %** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Positive Outlook** | Customer health is green. Good customer engagement. QBRs are routine. Executive sponsor engaged. | *Health Score ≥ 75 (Green)Active engagementRenewal conversation startedNo open risk flags* | **90%** |
| **Neutral** | Customer health is neutral. Engagement is not great. No strong feeling about the renewal either way. | *Health Score 50–74 (Yellow)Inconsistent engagementNo exec sponsor confirmedNo active risk flags* | **70%** |
| **At Risk** | Customer health is red. Engagement is very low. No contact. No exec sponsor. Active risk flags present. | *Health Score < 50 (Red)Low or no engagementActive risk flag(s)No exec sponsorRenewal objections raised* | **10%** |
| **Intent to Churn** | Formal churn notice has been received. Customer has explicitly stated intent not to renew. | *Churn notice receivedContract end date confirmedMid-Contract Cancellation SOP triggered if mid-term* | **0%** |

**SECTION 3 — FORECAST METHODOLOGY**

| **Forecast Window** | **How It's Built** |
| --- | --- |
| **Q1 + Q2 (Next 6 months)** | Built on actual Renewal Forecast in SFDC. CSMs manually forecast each account using the 4 categories above. Updated weekly by CSMs before team meetings. This is the high-confidence, operational forecast. |
| **Q3 + Q4 (6–12 months out)** | Built using Health Scores. Accounts with Green health scores are assumed Positive Outlook. Yellow = Neutral. Red = At Risk. This is a directional forecast — accuracy improves as accounts move closer to renewal and CSMs update their SFDC forecast. |
| **Weighted ARR Calculation** | Each account's ARR is multiplied by the weighted % for its forecast category:Positive Outlook ARR × 90%Neutral ARR × 70%At Risk ARR × 10%Intent to Churn ARR × 0%Total Weighted Forecast = sum of all weighted ARR values. This is the number CX leadership reports to the business. |

**⚠** The weighted forecast is the number that matters. Total ARR tells you the potential — weighted ARR tells you the reality. Leadership makes decisions from the weighted number.

**SECTION 4 — RENEWAL STAGES (NEW SFDC)**

**ℹ** These are the new SFDC renewal stages replacing the old Internal Discovery / Customer Engagement / Renewal Paperwork / Negotiation / Closed Won / Closed Lost structure.

| **Stage** | **What It Means** | **CSM Action** | **Timing** | **Old SFDC Stage** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Internal Discovery** | Default stage. CSM is aware of upcoming renewal and beginning internal preparation. | Review account health, update forecast category, note any risks or expansion opps. Discuss in normal call cadence — do not contact customer about renewal yet. | *90-day mark* | *Internal Discovery / Internal Alignment* |
| **Renewal Initiated with Customer** | CSM has begun the renewal conversation with the customer in normal call cadence. | Discuss renewal in QBR or scheduled call. Gauge sentiment. Note any pricing concerns or contract questions. | *90–60 days out* | *Customer Engagement* |
| **60 Day Notice** | Formal 60-day renewal notice sent to customer via email. | Send Renewal Outreach — 60 Day email template. Log in Chatter. Update SFDC stage. | *60 days before renewal* | *—* |
| **45 Day Notice** | No response to 60-day notice. Second notice sent. | Send Renewal Outreach — 45 Day email template. If no response, flag as Neutral or At Risk. | *45 days before renewal* | *—* |
| **30 Day Notice** | No response to 45-day notice. Third and final notice sent. | Send Renewal Outreach — 30 Day email template. Escalate to Anna if still no response. | *30 days before renewal* | *—* |
| **Renewal Paperwork** | Contract sent to customer. Customer reviewing terms, redlines, security review, etc. | Send contract. Follow up every 3–5 days. Flag any redlines or pricing negotiations. | *Any point — ideally 30+ days out* | *Renewal Paperwork* |
| **Negotiation** | Price, terms, or products are actively being negotiated. | Work with Anna / Sarah on negotiation strategy. Do not agree to terms outside your authority. | *Any point — ideally 30+ days out* | *Negotiation* |
| **Auto Renew Window** | Customer is within the 30-day auto-renew window or has acknowledged auto-renewal. | Confirm auto-renew details. Send confirmation email. Update SFDC. | *30 days out if triggered* | *—* |
| **DocuSign Sent** | DocuSign sent to customer (or customer sends their own DocuSign). | Track DocuSign status. Follow up if not signed within 3 business days. | *Ideally 10+ days before renewal* | *—* |
| **Closed Won** | Contract signed. Renewal complete. | Log in Chatter. Update SFDC. Celebrate with the customer. Move back to standard cadence. | *Renewal date* | *Closed Won* |
| **Closed Lost** | Customer has churned. Contract not renewed. | Log in Chatter. Complete churn report. Trigger offboarding process. | *Renewal date* | *Closed Lost* |

**SECTION 5 — FORECAST EXAMPLES**

**Example: 4-account renewal cohort for January 2026**

| **Account / Scenario** | **Renewal Stage** | **Forecast Category** | **ARR** | **Weighted ARR** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| *Green account. Exec sponsor active. QBRs running. No flags.* | Renewal Initiated with Customer | **Positive Outlook (90%)** | $24,000 | **$21,600** |
| *Yellow account. Low engagement. No exec sponsor. One resolved flag.* | 60 Day Notice | **Neutral (70%)** | $18,000 | **$12,600** |
| *Red account. No contact in 60 days. Active Financial risk flag.* | 45 Day Notice | **At Risk (10%)** | $36,000 | **$3,600** |
| *Customer has sent formal churn notice.* | Renewal Initiated with Customer | **Intent to Churn (0%)** | $12,000 | **$0** |
| **TOTAL** | | | **$90,000** | **$37,800** |

**ℹ** In this example: $90,000 of ARR is up for renewal. The weighted forecast is $37,800 — meaning leadership should plan around recovering $37,800 with high confidence. The gap between $90,000 and $37,800 is the work the CSM needs to do.

**SECTION 6 — CSM RESPONSIBILITIES**

| **Responsibility** | **What to Do** | **Frequency** | **Where in SFDC** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Update Renewal Forecast Category** | Set the correct forecast category (Positive Outlook / Neutral / At Risk / Intent to Churn) for every account with a renewal in the next 6 months. | *Weekly — before team meeting* | *Renewal Object → Forecast picklist* |
| **Update Renewal Stage** | Move accounts through renewal stages as they progress. Never leave an account in a stale stage. | *Weekly — or when stage changes* | *Renewal Object → Stage picklist* |
| **Participate in forecast team meetings** | Update forecasts before the meeting. Come prepared with status on any accounts that moved categories. Have a plan for any At Risk accounts. | *Weekly* | *SFDC Renewal Forecast Report* |
| **Execute save plans** | As strategies and save plans are established in team meetings, the CSM is responsible for executing them. Log all actions in Chatter. | *As needed* | *Chatter (SFDC)* |
| **Send renewal outreach emails** | Send 60, 45, and 30 day renewal notices using the approved templates. Log each send in Chatter. | *At 60, 45, 30 days* | *Comm Templates → Renewal Outreach* |
| **Log all renewal notes in Chatter** | Every renewal conversation, objection, and commitment must be logged in Chatter. Not Slack. | *After every interaction* | *Account → Chatter feed* |
| **Aim for better renewal terms** | Best effort should always be made to move the customer to more favorable renewal terms or increase ARR. | *At renewal* | *Renewal Object → Contract terms* |
