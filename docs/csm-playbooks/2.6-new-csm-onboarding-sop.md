**SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW**

| **Purpose** | Give every new CSM a structured, consistent onboarding experience that covers the tools, processes, playbooks, and people they need to be effective from day one. |
| --- | --- |
| **Owner** | Anna owns the onboarding process. The new CSM owns completing this document. |
| **Timeline** | 30-day ramp. Week 1: orientation and tools. Week 2: shadowing and playbooks. Week 3: first accounts. Week 4: independent with support. |
| **Escalation Path** | New CSM → Anna → Sarah |
| **Key Rule** | All notes in Chatter (SFDC) — NOT Slack. |
