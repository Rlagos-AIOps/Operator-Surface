**SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW**

| **Trigger** | Customer requests to cancel their contract before the end date |
| --- | --- |
| **Goal** | Hold the customer to their contract while demonstrating commitment to finding value — escalate appropriately if they push back |
| **Stakeholders** | CSM → Anna (Manager) → Sarah (SVP of CX)/Marcel (CFO) |
| **Escalation Path** | CSM → Anna → Sarah → Legal (if required) |
| **Key Rule** | **ALL notes about the account live in Chatter — NOT Slack** |

**SECTION 2 — PROCESS STEPS**

| **#** | **Step** | **Action** | **Owner** | **Timing / Resources** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **1** | **CSM Initial Response** | CSM responds to the customer via email. Message: "You have signed a contract that runs through [CONTRACT END DATE]. I am committed to helping you find value in your [Company] product." Simultaneously: Submit Risk Flag in SFDC. Log all notes in Chatter. | CSM | Same day as cancellation request Risk Flag SOP Email Template — At-Risk Outreach Chatter (SFDC) |
| **2** | **Customer Pushes Back** | CSM loops Anna into the email thread. CSM emails customer, cc's Anna: "I have escalated to my manager Anna." Anna replies with a similar message: "You are under contract until [DATE], let's work together to find value." | CSM + Anna | If customer responds negatively to Step 1 Chatter (SFDC) Email Template — Escalation |
| **3** | **Customer Pushes Back Again** | Anna replies and loops in Sarah. Anna says: "I have escalated and am looping in our SVP of CX." Sarah replies, cc's Marcel: "You are under contract — how can we work together to find you value?" | Anna + Sarah | If customer responds negatively to Step 2 Chatter (SFDC) |
| **4** | **Customer Pushes Back Again** | Marcel and Sarah decide if this goes to Legal or what happens next. Action is taken by Marcel, David, or Louis. | Marcel / David / Louis | If customer responds negatively to Step 3 Legal team Chatter (SFDC) |
| **5** | **Final Notice** | Leadership determines the final course of action — legal escalation, negotiated exit, or continued retention effort. | Marcel / Sarah | After Step 4 if no resolution Legal Chatter (SFDC) |

**⚠ Reminder:** All account notes must be logged in Chatter (SFDC) — not Slack. Risk Flag must be submitted at Step 1.
