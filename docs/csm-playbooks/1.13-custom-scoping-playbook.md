**SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW**

| **Trigger** | A request for custom services arrives via Chatter — either from Sales (new sale) or from a CSM (expansion opportunity). Two types of custom scope: CX Professional Services and Custom Integration Development. |
| --- | --- |
| **Two Branches** | Branch 1 — CX Services SOW: Training, re-training, enablement, custom CX work. Fully within the CX team. Anna or Rosemary scopes and builds. Sarah approves and sets the price. Branch 2 — Custom Integration SOW: Custom integration development. Requires Nelson and Engineering/Product to scope. Anna or Rosemary builds the SOW from Nelson's scope. Sarah approves and sets the price. |
| **Intake** | All custom scope requests must come in via Chatter on the relevant SFDC Opportunity. Chatter is the system of record. If a request comes via Slack, direct the requester to repost in Chatter on the correct Opportunity. |
| **SOW Approval** | Sarah approves all SOWs and sets the final price before anything goes to the customer or into the Order Form. No exceptions. |
| **Order Form** | Once Sarah posts the approved SOW in Chatter, the SKU is added to the Order Form in SFDC and the SOW is attached. The process is the same regardless of who sends the contract. |
| **Escalation Path** | CSM → Anna → Sarah → David/Louis (as needed) |
| **Key Rule** | All notes in Chatter — not Slack. |

**SECTION 3 — BRANCH 1: CX SERVICES SOW**

**CX Professional Services — training, re-training, enablement, custom CX work**

**✓** This branch is fully within the CX team. Anna or Rosemary scopes and builds the SOW. Sarah approves and prices it. See the Ray Recovery SOW (March 2026) as a real-world example of a re-training package.

| **#** | **Step** | **Action** | **Owner** | **Resources / Notes** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **1** | **Request comes in via Chatter** | A custom CX services request arrives via Chatter — either from Sales (new sale) or from a CSM (expansion opportunity). The request should be posted on the relevant SFDC Opportunity. If no Opportunity exists yet, the requester should create one before posting: - New sale: New Business Opportunity - Expansion: Expansion Opportunity (naming convention: Customer Name — Expansion — YYYY-MM-DD) If the request arrives via Slack, direct the requester to repost in Chatter on the correct Opportunity. Chatter is the system of record. | Requester (Sales or CSM) | *SFDC → Opportunity → Chatter Expansion Opp naming: Customer Name — Expansion — YYYY-MM-DD* |
| **2** | **Anna or Rosemary scopes and builds the SOW** | Anna or Rosemary reviews the request and scopes the work. Using the CX Services SOW Template, build the SOW with: - Background and purpose - Scope of work (specific deliverables and sessions) - Project team ([Company] roles + customer roles) - Timeline and schedule - Assumptions and out-of-scope items - Pricing (leave Total Fee blank until Sarah approves) Delete the template instruction box before sending to Sarah for review. | Anna or Rosemary | *CX Services SOW Template ([Company]\_SOW\_CX\_Services\_Template\_v2.docx) See Ray Recovery SOW as a real-world example* |
| **3** | **Sarah reviews, approves, and sets the price** | Sarah reviews the scoped SOW and sets the final price. Sarah approves: - Scope is correct and deliverable - Pricing reflects the work - Terms and assumptions are accurate Once approved, Sarah updates the Total Fee field with the final price and signs off. If changes are needed, Sarah sends back to Anna or Rosemary with notes before re-review. | Sarah | *SOW draft from Step 2 Chatter — confirm approval* |
| **4** | **Sarah posts in Chatter on the Opportunity** | Sarah posts the approved SOW directly in Chatter on the SFDC Opportunity, tagging the CSM or AE who will build the Order Form. Chatter post must include: - The approved SOW attached as a PDF - SKU name: Professional Services Custom - Exact price to input in the Order Form - Any relevant notes Example: 'Here is the SOW for retraining. SKU Name: Professional Services Custom, input price as $1,000. Include SOW in Order Form. @[CSM Name] — keep us posted.' | Sarah | *SFDC → Opportunity → Chatter See Chatter post format in Section 4* |
| **5** | **Add SKU to Order Form in SFDC and attach SOW** | The tagged person (CSM or AE) adds the correct SKU to the Order Form in SFDC: - SKU: Professional Services Custom - Price: override the $250 placeholder with the exact price from the Sarah-approved SOW - Attach the SOW PDF to the Order Form The process is the same regardless of whether Sales or CX is sending the contract. | CSM or AE | *SFDC → Order Form → Price Book SKU: Professional Services Custom Price: exact amount from SOW (override placeholder)* |
| **6** | **Contract sent and executed** | Contract (including SOW) is sent to the customer via DocuSign from SFDC. Once both parties have signed, the Opportunity moves to Closed Won. Finance confirms billing. Slack celebration channel pinged. 🎉 Log confirmation in Chatter. | CSM or AE | *SFDC → DocuSign SFDC → Opportunity → Closed Won Chatter — log confirmation* |
| **7** | **CX Services delivered** | Anna or Rosemary (or the assigned IS/CSM) delivers the scoped services per the SOW timeline. All delivery notes, session summaries, and customer confirmations logged in Chatter. If scope changes mid-delivery, flag to Sarah before agreeing to any changes with the customer. | Anna / Rosemary / IS | *SOW timeline and deliverables Chatter — log all delivery notes* |

**SECTION 5 — CHATTER POST FORMAT REFERENCE**

**ℹ** Sarah uses this format when posting the approved SOW in Chatter. The tagged person (CSM or AE) uses this as confirmation to build the Order Form.

| **Field** | **What to Include** |
| --- | --- |
| **Post on** | The Expansion Opportunity in SFDC — not the Account. If no Expansion Opp exists yet, create one first. |
| **Tag** | @CSM (or @AE if Sales-driven). Whoever needs to action the Order Form. |
| **SOW attached** | Yes — attach the signed-off SOW PDF directly to the Chatter post. |
| **SKU details** | State the SKU name and the exact price to input. Example: 'SKU Name: Professional Services Custom, input price as $1,000. Include SOW in Order Form.' |
| **Opportunity naming** | Follow the convention: Customer Name — Expansion — YYYY-MM-DD. Example: Ray Recovery - Expansion - 2026-04-17 |
| **Keep posted** | End with 'Keep us posted' or equivalent so the tagged person knows to follow up in Chatter. |

**Example Chatter post (from the Ray Recovery expansion):**

"Here is the SOW for retraining.

SKU Name: Professional Services Custom, input price as 1000. Include SOW in Order Form.

Keep us posted. Thanks @[CSM Name]"
