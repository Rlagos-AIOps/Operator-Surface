**SECTION 2 — PRE-SEND CHECKLIST & PERSONALIZATION GUIDE**

**⚠** Never send a blank template. Each email should include at least 2-3 specific, accurate personalization details. Generic renewal emails feel transactional — personalized ones feel like partnership.

Additionally, be sure to review a customer’s billing terms prior to sending. If they are already on annual payments, do not suggest they move from monthly to annual. If they’re on annual you can offer participation in a case study as an incentive to lock in their rate.

**Pre-send checklist — complete before sending any renewal outreach:**

**✔** Usage last 30 and 90 days — pull from SFDC Product Usage Score or dashboard

**✔** Feature adoption trends — which modules and features are actively used?

**✔** Which teams or departments are active in the platform

**✔** Any recent wins or positive feedback from the customer

**✔** Integration adoption — EHR, VoIP, billing integrations in use?

**✔** Are they using dashboards and reports?

**✔** Any support tickets resolved well and worth acknowledging

**✔** Were they part of any beta or new feature access?

**✔** Any open goals or projects you discussed in QBR or calls

**✔** Confirm renewal date, billing contact name and email, current plan details

**Personalization signal guide:**

| **Personalization Signal** | **Where to Find It / What to Include** |
| --- | --- |
| **Usage milestones** | How many workflows, leads, or activities completed in the last 30/90 days? |
| **Product adoption** | Which features is the team actively using? Admissions module? BD tracking? Reporting? |
| **Teams / departments active** | How many staff are logging in regularly? Which departments? |
| **ROI outcomes** | Any census growth, intake efficiency, or referral source conversion improvements visible in [Company] data? |
| **Workflow improvements** | Have they streamlined any processes since onboarding — intake, referral tracking, reporting? |
| **Integration adoption** | Are they using their EHR, VoIP, or billing integrations consistently? |
| **Dashboard / report usage** | Are they running reports? Using dashboards? This signals they're getting strategic value. |
| **Support interactions** | Any support tickets that were resolved well and worth acknowledging? |
| **Recent wins or feedback** | Anything the customer has said recently about value — in calls, Chatter, or QBRs? |
| **Beta / new features** | Were they part of any beta programs or early feature access? |
