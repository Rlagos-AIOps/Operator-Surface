**SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW**

**ℹ** Use when a customer has gone dark with no specific known risk or crisis. Warm and light — not urgent. Try calling first. If the call goes unanswered, send 3.9A. If no response after 5 days, send 3.9B. If still nothing — file a risk flag and loop Anna in.

| **When to Send** | Customer has been unresponsive for 2+ weeks with no known issue or risk flag. No crisis — just silence. |
| --- | --- |
| **Sent By** | CSM |
| **Tone** | Warm, low-pressure, curious. Leave the door wide open — no guilt, no urgency. |
| **Escalation** | No response after both variants → file Customer Adoption or Potential Churn Risk flag → escalate to Anna. |
| **Difference from 3.8** | 3.8 At-Risk Outreach is used when you know there is a problem. 3.9 Re-engagement is when you just haven't heard from them and want to check in. |

**SECTION 2 — EMAIL TEMPLATES**

| **TEMPLATE 3.9A — Re-engagement — Initial Outreach** | |
| --- | --- |
| **To** | Customer POC / Primary Contact |
| **Subject** | Checking in — hope things are going well! |
| **Body** | Hi [Customer Name],  I hope things have been going well on your end!  I realized we haven't connected in a little while and wanted to check in.How is the team getting on with [Company]? Is there anything on your plate right now where I could be helpful — whether that's a quick question, a refresher on a feature, or just a catch-up call?  I'd love to find 20 minutes to reconnect whenever it works for you.  [Calendar link or suggested times] Looking forward to hearing from you!  [CSM Name] Customer Success Manager, [Company] |
| **Tips** | *Keep it short and genuinely warm. No pressure, no urgency. Reference something specific if you can — a feature they were excited about, a goal they mentioned, or a recent product update relevant to them.* |

| **TEMPLATE 3.9B — Re-engagement — Gentle Nudge (No Response to 3.9A)** | |
| --- | --- |
| **To** | Customer POC / Primary Contact |
| **Subject** | Still thinking of you — happy to help whenever you're ready |
| **Body** | Hi [Customer Name],I know things get busy — just wanted to send one more note in case my last message got buried.I'm here whenever you need anything — even if it's just a quick question or a product update I think might be useful for your team. No agenda, just want to make sure you're getting value from [Company].Feel free to reply here or grab time on my calendar whenever works.[Calendar link]Take care,[CSM Name]Customer Success Manager, [Company] |
| **Tips** | *Send 5 business days after 3.9A with no response. Even shorter and lighter. If no response after this — file a Customer Adoption or Potential Churn Risk flag and loop in Anna.* |
